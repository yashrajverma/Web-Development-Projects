Corona Virus

To stop the spread of Corona Virus. We all must follow the certain precautions :-

1. Wash your hands frequently and thoroughly with handwash for atleast 20 seconds. Use alcohol based hand sanetizer ,if soap and water isn't available .

2. Cover your mouth and nose with handerchif and wear a mask always. Cover your mouth with elbow or tissue while sneezing. And wash your hands.

3. Clean and Disinfect Don't touch surfaces directly use a tissue or wear hand gloves . Regularly clean the surfaces which might spread the virus.

4. Avoid Close Contact Maintain the actual distance of 6 feet's or 2 meter's while talking or in a queue.
